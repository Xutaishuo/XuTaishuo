Our Story

Family-run with passion, expertise, and a love for flowers since 1999

Welcome to Flotsam Florist

For over 25 years, Flotsam Florist has been a beloved part of the Illyput community. What started as a small family passion project has grown into a trusted local business, bringing beauty and joy to countless homes and celebrations.

While we've been operating in Illyput for 9 years, our floral expertise spans more than a quarter-century. Every arrangement we create is infused with this wealth of experience, attention to detail, and genuine care.

Our Philosophy

Quality First

We source only the freshest, highest-quality flowers from trusted growers. Every stem is carefully selected to ensure longevity and beauty.

As a family-run business, we treat every customer like family. We take time to understand your needs and create arrangements with personal meaning.
